# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Elina, a young child, and family visitors opening small browser games on a phone, tablet, or desktop. They want to start playing quickly without setup, accounts, or explanation.

## Product Purpose

Elinas Welt is a static home for Elina's first web projects: playful drawing and thinking games that run directly in the browser. Success means each app feels obvious, sturdy, touchable, and fun within seconds.

## Brand Personality

Playful, direct, tactile. The interface should feel like colorful physical game pieces, not like a grown-up dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid grown-up SaaS polish, generic landing-page decoration, tiny fussy controls, muted gray-on-beige interfaces, and anything that requires instructions before play.

## Design Principles

- Start with the game, not the explanation.
- Use app two and app three (`bunte-bausteine`, `mastermind`) as the current design reference.
- Let app one (`formenmalen`) lag until it is updated into the same direction.
- Make controls big, high-contrast, and forgiving.
- Keep German copy short and child-readable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support keyboard and touch where the game allows it. Keep focus states visible, contrast strong, hit targets large, and motion optional or state-based. Do not rely on color alone for future game feedback.
